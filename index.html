MOVE THAT COUCH!

MOVE THAT COUCH! is a software platform that connects customers with independent local drivers for single-item or small-load furniture and goods delivery.

Customers submit delivery requests through the platform and receive quotes from drivers. Customers pay a booking deposit online to reserve a delivery. The remaining balance is paid directly to the driver.

Drivers are independent contractors and are not employees of MOVE THAT COUCH!.
