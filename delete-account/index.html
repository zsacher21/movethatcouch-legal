Account Deletion – MOVE THAT COUCH!

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly within the MOVE THAT COUCH! app:

  • Open the MOVE THAT COUCH! app
  • Go to the Account tab
  • Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and associated personal information are deleted immediately upon confirmation. No admin approval is required.

What data is deleted

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and profile photo
  • Saved addresses and business names
  • Notification preferences and device tokens
  • Payout method details
  • Service areas (for drivers)

What data may be retained

To comply with legal, tax, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution obligations, we may retain anonymized records of:

  • Completed delivery transactions
  • Payment and refund records
  • Dispute and moderation history

These records are associated with a generic "Deleted User" identifier and do not contain your name, email address, phone number, or other direct personal identifiers.

Certain transaction and financial records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by applicable tax, accounting, and fraud prevention laws.

Alternative method

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you may contact us at support@movethatcouch.com and request account deletion.
