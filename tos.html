3. Quotes, Booking Deposits, and Payments

3.1 Driver Quotes

Drivers set their own prices. Quotes may expire or be withdrawn before acceptance.

3.2 Booking Deposits

When a customer accepts a quote, a 20% booking deposit is charged through the platform using Stripe.

  • MOVE THAT COUCH! keeps the booking deposit on completed jobs.
  • The remaining 80% is paid directly to the driver by the customer, outside the app.

3.3 Cancellation Policy

If a customer cancels within 48 hours of the delivery window:

  • The driver receives 100% of the booking deposit.
  • Referral bonuses are not paid.

Important timing rule: The 48-hour window is calculated from the accepted quote at the time of booking. Later reschedules or chat discussions do not change this.

Stripe processes booking deposit refunds, late cancellation payouts, and referral bonuses.

4. Referral Program
