5. Quotes, Scheduling, Booking Deposits, and Payments

5.1 Driver Quotes & Scheduling Terms

Drivers independently set their own prices, availability, and delivery terms, including proposed dates and time windows. Customers and drivers may negotiate price, date, time, and scope directly through the platform prior to booking.

Quotes may expire if not accepted within the timeframe shown or may be withdrawn by the driver before acceptance.

5.2 Booking Deposits

When a customer accepts a quote, a 20% booking deposit based on the accepted quote is charged through the platform using Stripe.

  • MOVE THAT COUCH! keeps the booking deposit on completed jobs.
  • The remaining 80% is paid directly to the driver by the customer.

5.3 Rescheduling & Cancellation Policy

Customers and drivers may mutually agree to reschedule a booked delivery. However, for purposes of cancellation and refund eligibility, the original date and time agreed to at booking controls all cancellation logic.

If a customer cancels more than 48 hours before the originally booked delivery date and time, the booking deposit is refunded to the customer.

If a customer cancels within 48 hours of the originally booked delivery date and time, the driver receives 100% of the booking deposit.

If a driver cancels a booked delivery for any reason, the booking deposit is automatically refunded to the customer.

Important timing rule: The 48-hour window is calculated from the accepted quote at booking, regardless of later discussions or rescheduling.

Stripe processes booking deposit refunds and late cancellation payouts.
