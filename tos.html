5. Quotes, Booking, Payments & Scheduling

5.1 Driver Quotes & Negotiation

Drivers set their own prices and define their own terms, including availability, delivery date, time window, scope of work, and any conditions related to the delivery. Quotes are negotiated directly between the customer and the driver through the platform prior to booking.

Quotes may expire or be withdrawn before acceptance.

5.2 Booking Deposits

When a customer accepts a quote, a 20% booking deposit is charged through the platform using Stripe.

  • MOVE THAT COUCH! keeps the booking deposit on completed jobs.
  • The remaining 80% is paid directly to the driver.

5.3 Rescheduling & Cancellation Policy

Customers and drivers may mutually agree to reschedule a booked delivery. However, the original date and time agreed to at booking remains controlling for cancellation and deposit timing purposes unless otherwise stated in writing within the platform.

If a customer cancels within 48 hours of the originally booked delivery window:

  • The driver receives 100% of the booking deposit.
  • Referral bonuses are not paid.

Stripe processes booking deposit refunds and referral bonuses.

5.4 Receipts, Payment Records & Tax Documentation

Upon completion of a delivery, customers receive a platform receipt for the 20% booking deposit paid through MOVE THAT COUCH!. This receipt reflects only amounts processed in-app and does not include the remaining balance paid directly to the driver.

Drivers are independent service providers and may issue their own service receipts for amounts paid directly to them. MOVE THAT COUCH! does not verify, enforce, or guarantee the issuance or accuracy of driver-provided receipts.

Customers may request a W-9 form from drivers through the platform. Drivers are solely responsible for providing accurate and complete tax documentation. MOVE THAT COUCH! does not verify W-9s and is not liable if a driver fails to provide one.

6. Referral Program

Referrers earn 25% of the booking deposit when a referred user completes a delivery. Referral bonuses are paid only after successful job completion and are not paid for canceled or incomplete deliveries.
