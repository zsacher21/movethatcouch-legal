1. Platform Purpose & Relationship of the Parties

MOVE THAT COUCH! is a technology platform that connects customers and independent drivers for furniture and small-load deliveries. We do not provide transportation services and do not operate as a carrier.

Drivers are independent providers and are not employees, agents, or contractors of MOVE THAT COUCH! All agreements, interactions, quotes, payments, and deliveries are solely between customers and drivers. The platform is not responsible for loss, damage, or delays.

The platform facilitates connections only and does not arrange, manage, or provide shipping, freight, or logistics services.

1.5 Where the Platform Operates

MOVE THAT COUCH! is available in all 50 U.S. states and in U.S. territories, including Puerto Rico, Guam, the U.S. Virgin Islands, the Northern Mariana Islands, and American Samoa.

The platform is intended solely for deliveries that can be completed under the continuous custody of a single independent driver using standard road travel, where a drivable route exists between the pickup and drop-off locations.

Deliveries that require international transit, customs processing, air or ocean transport, or handoff to another carrier or logistics provider are not supported.

Availability of the platform may vary by location or route. MOVE THAT COUCH! reserves the right to restrict or deny certain routes.

1.6 Items Not Supported on the Platform

MOVE THAT COUCH! is designed primarily for furniture and appliance moves. Other items may be allowed, provided they do not fall into the categories below.

The platform does not support requests involving:

Passenger Transportation

  • Transporting people as passengers
  • Ride-along requests
  • Providing a ride to or from a pickup or delivery location

MOVE THAT COUCH! is not a ride or passenger transportation service.

Live Animals

  • Live animals of any kind (pets, livestock, wildlife)
  • Animal transport containers containing animals

Illegal or Prohibited Items

  • Illegal drugs or controlled substances
  • Stolen property
  • Counterfeit or illicit goods
  • Any item whose transport is illegal under applicable law

Hazardous or Regulated Materials

  • Fuel, gasoline, diesel, or oil
  • Propane tanks or compressed gas
  • Chemicals, solvents, pesticides, or flammable liquids
  • Fireworks or explosives
  • Medical waste or biohazardous materials
  • Industrial or large lithium battery packs

Weapons

  • Firearms
  • Ammunition
  • Firearm parts
  • Explosives or other weapons prohibited by law

Vehicles, Boats, and Aircraft

  • Automobiles, trucks, or vans
  • Motorcycles or roadway vehicles
  • Boats, jet skis, or personal watercraft
  • Aircraft or aircraft parts
  • Trailers or towable equipment being transported as cargo
  • Requests involving towing, vehicle recovery, or operation of vehicles as part of the job

This restriction applies to items or services being requested. Drivers may independently use their own vehicles, trucks, vans, or trailers as part of their equipment.

Structures Requiring Specialized Handling

  • Sheds, garages, or permanent structures
  • Tiny homes or buildings
  • Jobs requiring permits, cranes, or specialized rigging
