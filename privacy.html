Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MOVE THAT COUCH! (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the MOVE THAT COUCH! platform.

MOVE THAT COUCH! is owned and operated by ZM Platforms LLC, a Texas limited liability company.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Stripe account connection details (for drivers or users receiving payouts)
  • Service area center points and delivery radiuses (for drivers)

1.2 Delivery Information

  • Pickup and drop-off addresses and approximate locations
  • Item descriptions and photos
  • Preferred delivery dates or time windows
  • Quotes sent and received
  • Booking deposit status and related transaction details
  • In-app chat messages

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and general region
  • Basic usage information

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Connect customers with drivers
  • Process booking deposits, refunds, referral bonuses, and late cancellation payouts through Stripe
  • Enable in-app messaging
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell personal information or share it for third-party marketing.

3. Email & SMS Notifications

We send transactional email and SMS notifications related to platform activity, such as booking confirmations, delivery updates, and cancellations.

Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

4. Payments & Stripe

MOVE THAT COUCH! uses Stripe to process booking deposits, refunds, late cancellation fees paid to drivers, and referral bonuses paid to users.

The remaining portion of an accepted quote (typically 80%) is paid directly from the customer to the driver outside of the platform. MOVE THAT COUCH! does not collect or process that portion.

We do not store full credit card numbers or bank account details on our servers. Payment information is handled directly by Stripe in accordance with its security and privacy practices.

5. Location Information

Location data is used only to support delivery requests and matching.

6. Data Retention & Security

Information is retained as reasonably necessary to operate the platform and comply with legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards to protect data.

7. Children’s Privacy

MOVE THAT COUCH! is not intended for users under 18.
