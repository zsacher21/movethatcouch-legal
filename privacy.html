By creating an account or using the platform, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Payout method for referral bonuses (Venmo, Zelle, or PayPal – no CashApp)
  • Service area center points and delivery radiuses (for drivers)

1.2 Delivery Information

  • Pickup and drop-off addresses and approximate locations
  • Item descriptions and photos
  • Preferred delivery dates or time windows
  • Quotes sent and received
  • Booking deposit status and related transaction details
  • In-app chat messages between customers and drivers

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and general region
  • Basic usage information

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Connect customers with drivers
  • Process booking deposits and referral payouts
  • Enable in-app messaging
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve platform safety and reliability
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell personal information or share it for third-party marketing.

3. In-App Messaging & Contact Details

Users communicate through MOVE THAT COUCH! messaging tools. Phone numbers and email addresses are not shared directly.

4. Email & SMS Notifications

MOVE THAT COUCH! may send transactional notifications via email and SMS, including booking confirmations, delivery updates, reminders, and support messages.

SMS messages are strictly transactional and related to platform activity only. Message frequency varies.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

5. Payments & Referral Payouts

Deposits are processed by third-party providers such as Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers.

6. Location Information

Location data is used only to support delivery requests and matching.

7. How We Share Information

Information is shared only with matched users, service providers, or when legally required.

8. Data Retention

Information is retained as reasonably necessary to operate the platform and comply with law.

9. Security
