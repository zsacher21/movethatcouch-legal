MOVE THAT COUCH! – Privacy Policy Last Updated: December 2025 MOVE THAT COUCH! (“we,” “our,” or “the platform”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the MOVE THAT COUCH! app or website. By creating an account or using the platform, you agree to the practices described below. 1. Information We Collect We collect the following information when you create an account or use the platform: 1.1 Account Information Name Phone number Email address Profile photo (optional) Payout method for referral bonuses (Venmo, Zelle, or PayPal — no CashApp) Service area center points and delivery radiuses (for drivers) 1.2 Delivery Information Pickup and drop-off locations Item descriptions and photos Preferred delivery window Chat messages exchanged with other users Quotes sent and received Payment status for booking deposits 1.3 Technical Information We may automatically collect: Device type Browser type IP address Basic interaction data (e.g., pages viewed, features used) This helps us operate and improve the platform but is not used for advertising. 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Create and manage your MOVE THAT COUCH! account Connect customers with drivers Process booking deposits Send referral bonuses Facilitate in-app messaging Provide customer support Improve app performance and reliability Prevent fraud or misuse of the platform We do not sell your data and do not use it for targeted advertising. 3. In-App Messaging & Privacy To protect privacy: Phone numbers and email addresses are never shared with other users All communication is routed through in-app messaging only Messages may be monitored or reviewed if needed to enforce safety or prevent abuse If a user updates their account information while a delivery is in progress, the platform will continue using the information associated with that delivery until the job is completed This ensures deliveries are not disrupted by mid-job changes. 4. Payments & Deposits MOVE THAT COUCH! processes only the 20% booking deposit through the platform. The deposit is paid to MOVE THAT COUCH! on completed jobs The remaining 80% is paid directly to the driver, outside the app Payment details for Venmo/Zelle/PayPal are collected only for referral payouts We do not store or process full credit card numbers; payments are handled by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe) 5. Referral Program Information If you participate in the referral program, we store: Who referred whom Referral payout method Whether a first job was completed No referral bonus is paid if a job is canceled or not completed. 6. Location Information For customers: Pickup and drop-off addresses are used only for the delivery you request. For drivers: “Service area center points” and radiuses help determine which requests you see. Exact addresses are shared only after a customer accepts a quote and pays the deposit. Before acceptance, drivers see only an approximate location. 7. How We Share Information We only share information in the following situations: 7.1 To complete a delivery Customers and drivers see each other’s names, profile photos (if added), and necessary delivery details Exact addresses are shared only after a quote is accepted 7.2 Service Providers We may share information with: Payment processors Hosting providers Customer support tools They may only use this information to perform services on our behalf. 7.3 Legal Requirements We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, or court order. We do not sell personal information to third parties. 8. Data Retention We retain data only as long as necessary to: Maintain your account Comply with legal requirements Resolve disputes Enforce platform rules You may request that we delete your account data at any time, subject to required retention (e.g., payments, fraud prevention). 9. Security We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. However, no online platform is perfectly secure, and users agree to use MOVE THAT COUCH! at their own risk. 10. Children’s Privacy MOVE THAT COUCH! is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available on our website and within the app. By continuing to use the platform, you agree to the updated Policy. 12. Contact Information For privacy questions or data requests, please contact: support@movethatcouch.com
